How to Play Dr. Robby

  1. Move: Arrow keys or WASD to walk to stations.
  2. Triage with context: Immediate/Emergent clues should move first, but you have time to think.
  3. Flow every case: Exam -> Stabilize -> Medication or Procedure -> Disposition.
  4. Codes are earned: Severe neglected patients can crash if delayed, then must go straight to OR.
  5. Disposition matters: Discharge only complete/stable cases; admit those needing ongoing care.
  6. Wrong destination hurts: Unsafe discharge bounces back; unnecessary admission gets rejected.
